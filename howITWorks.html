How StreetMitra Works

A simple rescue roadmap connecting citizens, volunteers, NGOs, and emergency responders to help street animals quickly and efficiently.

Rescue Roadmap

Every rescue follows a structured flow to ensure animals receive quick medical attention and safe support.

1
➜
📍

Report The Animal

A citizen notices an injured or sick street dog and submits a rescue report using the StreetMitra platform.

2
➜
📸

Upload Details

Photos, videos, location, audio notes, and emergency descriptions are added for accurate rescue coordination.

3
➜
🚑

Emergency Verification

The rescue team reviews the report, checks urgency level, and verifies the case.

4
➜
🤝

NGO & Volunteer Connection

Nearby NGOs and rescue volunteers are notified and assigned to respond to the emergency.

5
➜
🏥

Medical Assistance

The rescued animal receives treatment, medication, shelter support, and recovery care.

6
➜
🐾

Recovery & Follow-Up

Volunteers monitor recovery progress and ensure the animal remains safe and cared for.

Together We Save Lives

StreetMitra creates a bridge between people who care and the rescue teams capable of helping animals in need. Every report, volunteer, and rescue effort contributes to a safer life for street animals.
