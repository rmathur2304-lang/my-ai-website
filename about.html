About StreetMitra

StreetMitra is a student-driven rescue and coordination platform helping injured, abandoned, and sick street dogs connect with rescue volunteers and trusted NGOs across Jaipur.

Who We Are

We are a compassionate team of students, volunteers, coordinators, and animal lovers working together to create faster rescue response systems for street animals. Our mission is simple: no injured street dog should suffer without help.
